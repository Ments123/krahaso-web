# What Krahaso is

Krahaso is a grocery price-comparison and rewards app for Kosovo.

Users can:

* Compare grocery prices between supermarkets
* Create a shopping basket
* See which supermarket offers the lowest total basket price
* Scan a product barcode to compare it
* Photograph a supermarket receipt after shopping
* Earn points from verified purchases
* Exchange points for rewards, discounts and offers

The simple product journey is:

**Krahaso. Skano. Fito.**

## Krahaso

Create a grocery basket and see where it costs less.

## Skano

Scan a product barcode before buying or photograph the receipt after shopping.

## Fito

Earn points from eligible purchases and unlock rewards.

The website should make this understandable within the first few seconds.
